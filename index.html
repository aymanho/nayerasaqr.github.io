THE STORY BEHIND THE VOICE

Hello, I'm Nayera Sakr

For me, voice-over is more than a profession. It's the art of transforming words into experiences.

Working in both Arabic (Modern Standard Arabic & Egyptian Arabic) and English (Neutral & American accents), I help brands tell their stories, creators bring ideas to life, and audiences connect with messages that matter. Whether it's a commercial, audiobook, documentary, IVR, or dubbing project, every performance is shaped by a genuine passion for storytelling and a dedication to the finest details.

The goal is simple: to create a voice that feels authentic, resonates with listeners, and leaves a lasting impression.

Your words already have meaning—let's give them a voice. If you're looking for a female voice artist to bring personality, emotion, and authenticity to your project, let's work together.
